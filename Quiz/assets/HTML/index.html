Trivia Facts

Discover fascinating facts to broaden your knowledge.

The Eiffel Tower's Secret Apartment

Did you know that the Eiffel Tower in Paris has a secret apartment at its peak? Built for Gustave Eiffel, the tower's designer, it was used to entertain guests. Although not open to the public, it adds a mysterious touch to this iconic landmark.

Honey Never Spoils

Honey found in ancient Egyptian tombs thousands of years old is still edible! Due to its low water content and acidic pH, honey has natural preservatives, making it virtually immortal. A sweet reminder of the lasting nature of this delicious treat.

The Great Wall of China Isn't Visible from the Moon

Despite the popular belief, astronauts have stated that the Great Wall of China is not visible from the moon with the naked eye. It's a fascinating myth-buster about one of the world's most iconic structures.
